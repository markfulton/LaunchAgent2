# ICP & Positioning

## Ideal Customer Profile
- **Primary audience:** Instagram influencers and creators with an established following who need to ship more reels without burning time editing captions or hashtags.[^source]
- **Secondary segments:**
  - Coaches, mentors, and course creators relying on Instagram to convert followers into clients.[^source]
  - Social media managers at lifestyle and wellness brands expanding their creator partnerships.[^source]
- **Key pains:**
  - Spending too long drafting high-performing hooks and captions for Trial Reels.[^source]
  - Fear of hurting engagement metrics by testing unproven ideas on their main feed.[^source]
  - Difficulty staying on top of trending sounds and hashtags that actually drive discovery.[^source]

## Positioning Statement
For Instagram creators who want to grow faster with Trial Reels, **Trial Reel Machine** is the only AI platform built specifically for Trial Reel campaigns. It creates complete reel packages with five smart variations, strategic hashtags, and live Instagram-style previews so creators can test, learn, and publish winners confidently without guesswork.[^source]

## Proof Points
- Generates hooks, descriptions, hashtags, and creative direction tuned for Instagram’s algorithmic discovery surfaces.[^source]
- Includes a curated viral reel video library plus upcoming AI video generation for even faster production.[^source]
- Aligns with Instagram’s 24-hour Trial Reel insight window and auto-publish workflow, making optimization data-driven instead of anecdotal.[^source]
