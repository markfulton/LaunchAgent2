# FAQ

**What are Instagram Trial Reels?**
Trial Reels let you publish to non-followers first, gather 24-hour performance insights, and auto-publish winning reels to your entire audience.[^source]

**How does Trial Reel Machine help?**
It generates complete reel packages—hooks, captions, hashtags, creative direction, and five smart variations—so you can iterate quickly without manual copywriting.[^source]

**Can I preview the content before posting?**
Yes. Every generation includes an Instagram-style preview showing handles, engagement counters, and layout, plus one-click copy for each field.[^source]

**Do I need my own video footage?**
You can use your own clips or download from the built-in viral reel video library. AI video generation is on the roadmap.[^source]

**Is there a limit on how many reels I can generate?**
No. The Unlimited Creator Plan includes unlimited generations for $14.95/month (or $144/year) with cancel-anytime flexibility.[^source]

**Will this affect my Instagram account safety?**
Trial Reel Machine operates independently from Instagram/Meta. You still post natively, so you retain full control and follow platform guidelines.[^source]

**Who is this best for?**
Creators, influencers, coaches, and social teams who rely on Instagram growth and need to test multiple Trial Reels daily without burning time on copy.[^source]
