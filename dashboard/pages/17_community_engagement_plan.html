17. Community Engagement Plan

# Community Engagement Plan

## Objectives
- Spark conversations with Instagram creators about Trial Reel workflows.
- Drive sign-ups for the Unlimited Creator Plan.
- Collect feedback for roadmap items like AI video generation and analytics.

## Channels & Tactics
1. **Instagram DMs & Stories**
   - Share short loom demos showing how Trial Reel Machine generates five variations in seconds.[^source]
   - Use story polls asking, "How many Trial Reels do you post each week?" with swipe-up to the landing page.

2. **Creator Communities (Discord, Slack groups)**
   - Host AMAs on how to interpret Trial Reel 24-hour insights and set auto-publish thresholds.[^source]
   - Offer exclusive templates for niche audiences (wellness, business, lifestyle) to encourage referrals.

3. **Weekly Live Sessions**
   - Run a 30-minute live build every Thursday: collect prompts from attendees, generate reels, and discuss why certain hooks win.[^source]
   - Clip highlights into short reels using the viral video library for social reposts.

4. **Email & Newsletter Integration**
   - Share "Trial Reel Wins of the Week" featuring anonymized metrics and the winning hooks generated by the platform.[^source]
   - Encourage replies with requests for new tone presets or features.

5. **Feedback Loop**
   - Offer a "Roadmap Insider" tag for creators who submit ideas on AI video or analytics depth; they receive early access to betas.[^source]
   - Aggregate top feature requests in monthly update posts to keep the community informed.

## Success Metrics
- DM reply rate and number of sample generations requested.
- Live session attendance and conversion to paid plan.
- Volume of roadmap suggestions collected per month.

[^source]: [Trial Reel Machine homepage](https://trialreelmachine.com/)
