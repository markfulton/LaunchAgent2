# Sales Outreach Script (Instagram DM)

1. **Warm opener**
   - "Hey {{Name}} – love how you’re testing new reel concepts lately. Your {{recent series}} caught my eye."
2. **Problem acknowledgment**
   - "Trial Reels are gold for growth, but drafting five versions of every hook and caption is a time sink."
3. **Value proposition**
   - "I built Trial Reel Machine to do that heavy lifting. Describe your idea once and it generates hooks, captions, hashtags, and five smart variations—plus an Instagram-style preview you can copy in one click."[^source]
4. **Social proof**
   - "Creators across wellness, business, and lifestyle niches are using it to test multiple trial reels daily without cluttering their grid."[^source]
5. **CTA**
   - "Want to see what it would do with your next idea? I can spin up a sample or you can grab unlimited access for $14.95/month here."[^source]
6. **Close**
   - "Either way, keep crushing it. Happy to share more trial reel tips if you’re iterating on a launch." – Mark

[^source]: [Trial Reel Machine homepage](https://trialreelmachine.com/)
